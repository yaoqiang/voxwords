## EN — FAQs

### How does VoxWords work?
Hold the mic and say a word in your native language. We generate a card in the learning language, and you can tap to hear pronunciation.

### Why is translation unavailable sometimes?
VoxWords uses on-device translation (iOS 18+). Some language pairs require downloading a language pack. The system may continue downloading in the background.

### Why is audio volume sometimes low?
If you just recorded speech, iOS audio session modes can affect playback volume. VoxWords automatically switches back to the best playback mode before speaking.

### Does VoxWords work offline?
If the needed translation language packs are installed, translation can work offline. Speech recognition may still depend on device support.

### Which languages are supported?
Currently: Chinese, English, Japanese, and Korean.

## 中文 — 常见问题

### VoxWords 怎么用？
按住麦克风说一个母语词汇，我们会生成学习语言的卡片，并可一键听发音。

### 为什么有时提示翻译不可用？
VoxWords 使用 iOS 18+ 的系统离线翻译能力。有些语言对需要先下载语言包。即使你关闭系统下载弹窗，语言包也可能继续在后台下载。

### 为什么有时播放声音变小？
录音后系统音频模式可能影响播放音量。VoxWords 会在播放前自动切回更适合的播放模式。

### 能离线使用吗？
如果所需的翻译语言包已安装，翻译可以离线使用；语音识别能力取决于设备与系统支持。

### 支持哪些语言？
目前支持：中文、英文、日语、韩语。
